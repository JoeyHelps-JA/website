Maria Chaplick

Registered Psychotherapist

Maria Chaplick
Email: maria@nextsteppsychotherapy.com
Phone: 647-614-1361
Where to find them: 720 Spadina Avenue or Virtual
Education: Master of Arts in Theology: Spiritual Care and Psychotherapy, Wilfrid Laurier University.

Biography: I provide psychotherapy for people experiencing a variety of issues, including those related to anxiety, anger, depression, and self-image. My approach to therapy is collaborative and I help people develop a new relationship to their thoughts and emotions, providing them with a renewed sense of agency. I do short-term and long-term work with clients, focusing on effective coping skills and exploring certain beliefs and experiences. I help people cultivate self-compassion to respond to themselves in more helpful and effective ways. I start with where you are and we go together from there to help you be the person you want to be.
